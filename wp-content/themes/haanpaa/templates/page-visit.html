Your first visit

What to expect when you walk in.

  1. Step 01 Park and walk up

    Free parking on-site. The front door is unlocked thirty minutes before each class. Come in, sign in at the desk, and a coach will say hi.

  2. Step 02 Loaner gear, every time

    A clean gi and gloves are waiting for you. Athletic clothes underneath, bare feet on the mat. No purchase required for your trial.

  3. Step 03 A coach takes you through

    A coach will pair you with a senior student and walk through warmups, the day's technique, and an optional first roll. We pace it to you, not the room.

  4. Step 04 Sit-down, no upsell

    After class, the head coach will sit with you for ten minutes. Ask anything. Plans, pricing, schedule fit. We never pressure you on the spot.
