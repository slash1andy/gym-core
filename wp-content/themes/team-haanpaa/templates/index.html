Our Academy

Where the mat meets the heart

Haanpaa Martial Arts is more than an academy. We are a close-knit family of practitioners dedicated to Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and kickboxing. From your very first class to your black belt journey, we walk the path together.

Family First

We are a team built on mutual respect, encouragement, and togetherness. Every member is family from day one.

Discipline

Consistency on the mat translates to consistency in life. We cultivate discipline through structure and accountability.

Growth

Every roll, every drill, every class is an opportunity to grow — technically, mentally, and as a human being.

Community

Our doors are open to everyone. We believe martial arts has the power to bring people together and strengthen bonds.
