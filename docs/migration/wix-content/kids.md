# Kids Martial Arts

## Rockford, IL

## Confidence, Discipline, Integrity

Confidence, Discipline, Integrity - that is our focus. We want our students to have confidence in their individual value -- to know they are worth sticking up for! This is not our only mission. Confidence goes well beyond self defense and into work ethic and self control. We have seen time and time again, if a student's confidence and discipline goes up so does their ability to focus and follow direction.

## Kids Jiu-Jitsu (Ages 7-12)

Kids Jiu-Jitsu specializes in ground fighting and essential self defense techniques. Specifically the art is centered around Grappling, Throws, Sweeps, and other immobilizing techniques. Since most physical altercations end up on the ground Jiu-Jitsu is great for preparing children for the most difficult and imminent self defense scenarios. Similar to some aspects of wrestling most students find the environment fun and love learning the techniques and discipline alongside their class mates.

## JR. Ninjas (Ages 5-7)

The Jr. Ninjas is where we can apply the same self defense principles offered in the kids Jiu-Jitsu Program, but done in a format that works specifically for our younger students!
