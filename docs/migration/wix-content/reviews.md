## Testimonials

### Leina L.

Ok, so let me start out by saying I have been to SEVERAL martial arts gyms. Some were awesome some just...not. This gym is awesome! I've been training with Coach Darby for a couple years now and he is everything you look for in a coach. He's very understanding, patient, helpful, knowledgeable, trust worthy, and very well qualified. Classes with him are fun and enjoyable...and so are the people! I have been to gyms where the people were so awful I just could not bring myself to go back. But with Team Haanpaa it's not just people it's family. I always leave smiling and laughing because it's just a blast. As a coach he's A+. He breaks down moves for you so they are easier to understand. However, if you still don't quite have the hang of it he will break it down even more! It's amazing...and not only is HE very helpful but the students are too, they never act like a bunch ok know it all jerks. Everyone there is super respectful. The last thing I'd like to say is that if you are a woman please don't be afraid to try classes. Normally, classes like these are male dominated but please do not let that deter you from trying it out. Everyone there is very respectful of each other and very respectful of feelings. This is a close contact sport and I think that it keeps a lot of women from trying it out, but it shouldn't because it's so much fun and VERY helpful in self defense situations! There are other girls there but partners get switched and some women get scared. But I promise you there is nothing to be scared about! No one is there to be mean or creepy, it's all about learning! So come try it out..whether it's for fun, general exercise, learning, self defense, or for competition I promise you won't regret it. It's a blast!!
