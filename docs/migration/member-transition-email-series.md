# Member Transition Email Series

> 3-email sequence for notifying members about the Spark-to-WooCommerce migration.
> All emails use MailPoet/AutomateWoo merge tags. Replace `{gym_name}`, `{location}`,
> `{first_name}`, `{portal_url}`, `{support_email}`, `{support_phone}`, and
> `{billing_date}` with real values before sending.

---

## Email 1 — "We're upgrading our systems" (2 weeks before transition)

**Subject:** Big news: we're upgrading your member experience

**Trigger:** MailPoet scheduled newsletter sent to all active subscribers, filtered by
`gym_location` taxonomy. Schedule 14 days before the cutover date.

**Body:**

```
Hi {first_name},

We have exciting news — {gym_name} is upgrading our membership system to give you a
better experience. Over the next couple of weeks, we're moving to a new platform that
will make managing your account easier than ever.

WHAT'S CHANGING:
- You'll get a new online member portal where you can view your schedule, track your
  rank progress, and manage your account.
- Your billing will move to our new secure payment system.

WHAT'S STAYING THE SAME:
- Your membership plan and pricing — nothing changes.
- Your rank, belt history, and attendance records — everything transfers.
- Your class schedule at {location} — same times, same instructors, same training.

WHAT YOU NEED TO DO RIGHT NOW:
- Nothing! We'll handle everything on our end.

We'll send you another email next week with instructions for accessing your new member
portal. If you have any questions in the meantime, just reply to this email or call us
at {support_phone}.

Train hard,
The {gym_name} Team
```

---

## Email 2 — "Your new member portal is ready" (1 week before transition)

**Subject:** Your new {gym_name} member portal is ready

**Trigger:** MailPoet scheduled newsletter, 7 days before cutover. Alternatively,
trigger via AutomateWoo workflow when the member's WooCommerce account is created
(hook: `gym_core_member_migrated`).

**Body:**

```
Hi {first_name},

Your new member portal is live and ready for you! Here's everything you need to know.

LOG IN TO YOUR NEW PORTAL:
1. Go to {portal_url}
2. Click "Lost your password?" to set your password (use the email address we have
   on file for you).
3. Once logged in, you'll see your membership details, class schedule, rank history,
   and more.

YOUR PAYMENT METHOD:
- For security, saved payment methods do not transfer between systems.
- When you log in, you'll be prompted to enter your payment card. This is a one-time
  step — your card will be securely saved for future billing.
- Your first billing cycle on the new system will be on {billing_date}.

YOUR MEMBERSHIP:
- Plan: same as today, same price.
- Rank and belt history: fully transferred.
- Attendance records: carried over.

If you run into any trouble logging in, just reply to this email, call us at
{support_phone}, or stop by the front desk at {location}. We're happy to help.

See you on the mats,
The {gym_name} Team
```

---

## Email 3 — "You're all set" (day of transition)

**Subject:** You're all set — welcome to your new {gym_name} portal

**Trigger:** AutomateWoo workflow triggered when the member's Spark subscription is
deactivated and WooCommerce subscription status is `active`
(hook: `gym_core_transition_complete`). Alternatively, send as a MailPoet scheduled
newsletter on the cutover date.

**Body:**

```
Hi {first_name},

The transition is complete — your membership at {location} is now running on our new
system!

HERE'S WHAT'S SET:
- Your membership is active and your account is ready.
- Your next billing date is {billing_date}.
- Your rank, attendance history, and badges have all been transferred.

QUICK LINKS:
- Member portal: {portal_url}
- Class schedule: {portal_url}/classes
- Account & billing: {portal_url}/my-account

NEED HELP?
If anything looks off or you have questions, we're here for you:
- Email: {support_email}
- Phone: {support_phone}
- Or just talk to us at the front desk.

Thank you for being part of the {gym_name} family. We're excited about this upgrade
and we think you'll love the new experience.

See you in class,
The {gym_name} Team
```

## AutomateWoo / MailPoet Implementation Notes

| Email | Recommended Tool | Trigger / Timing |
|-------|-----------------|------------------|
| Email 1 | MailPoet newsletter | Scheduled send, T-14 days |
| Email 2 | AutomateWoo workflow or MailPoet | `gym_core_member_migrated` hook or scheduled T-7 days |
| Email 3 | AutomateWoo workflow | `gym_core_transition_complete` hook or scheduled on cutover day |

All emails should:
- Be sent from a recognizable sender name (e.g., the gym name, not "noreply")
- Have a plain-text fallback auto-generated by MailPoet/AutomateWoo
- Exclude members who have already churned or cancelled before the transition
- Be logged in the CRM contact timeline (Jetpack CRM activity log)
