# Member Guide — What You See as a Gym Member

This guide walks you through everything you can do as a member of Haanpaa Martial Arts through the website and in-gym systems. Whether you train at Rockford or Beloit, the experience works the same way.

---

## My Account Dashboard

When you log in and visit My Account, your dashboard shows a snapshot of your membership and training progress.

### Membership Card

At the top of the dashboard you will see your membership card. It displays:

- **Plan name** — the membership tier you are enrolled in (e.g., Adult BJJ Unlimited)
- **Status** — Active, On Hold, or Cancelled
- **Next billing date** — when your next payment will be charged
- **Amount** — your recurring payment amount

### Upcoming Classes

Below the membership card, you will see your upcoming classes for the next 7 days, filtered to your home location. This gives you a quick look at what is on the schedule without leaving the dashboard.

### Belt Rank Display

Your current belt rank is shown with:

- A **color dot** representing your belt color
- **Stripe dots** — filled dots for stripes you have earned, empty dots for stripes remaining before the next belt
- Your **program name** (e.g., Adult BJJ, Kids BJJ)

If you train in multiple programs, each rank is displayed separately.

### Foundations Status

If you are a new Adult BJJ student in the Foundations program, your dashboard includes a Foundations section showing:

- Your current **phase** (1, 2, or 3)
- **Classes completed** toward the current phase requirement
- **Coach rolls completed** (Phase 2)
- Overall progress toward Foundations completion

This section disappears once you have cleared Foundations.

### Gamification Summary

Your training stats are displayed in a summary section:

- **Total classes attended** — your all-time check-in count
- **Current week streak** — how many consecutive weeks you have trained, along with status (Active, Frozen, or Broken)
- **Badges earned** — a count and preview of your badge collection

### Quick Links

The dashboard provides direct links to:

- **Payment Methods** — update your card on file
- **Billing History** — view past payments and invoices
- **Class Schedule** — jump to the full schedule page

## Location Selection

Haanpaa Martial Arts has two locations: Rockford, IL and Beloit, WI. When you first visit the site, a banner prompts you to select your home location.

Once you choose, your selection is saved to your account (or via a browser cookie if you are not logged in). From that point forward:

- The class schedule filters to show only your location's classes
- Membership products display pricing and availability for your location
- Dashboard widgets show data relevant to your location

You can change your location at any time using the location selector on the site. If you train at both locations, you can switch freely — your attendance and progress are tracked across both.

## Membership Purchase

To sign up for a membership:

1. Browse the membership page. Products are automatically filtered to your selected location.
2. Choose your program (Adult BJJ, Kids BJJ, Kickboxing) and membership tier.
3. Proceed to checkout. Payment is handled through WooPayments, which supports credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
4. After checkout, you are automatically enrolled in a recurring membership plan.
5. Access is granted immediately — you can start attending classes right away.

Your membership appears under My Account > Subscriptions as soon as the payment processes.

---

## Subscription Management

You manage your membership under My Account > Subscriptions. From there you can:

- **View status** — see whether your subscription is Active, On Hold, or Cancelled
- **Check next payment date** — know when your next charge is coming
- **Upgrade or downgrade** — switch between membership tiers (e.g., move from 2x/week to Unlimited)
- **Cancel** — end your recurring membership
- **Update payment method** — change the card on file for this subscription

Changes take effect according to your billing cycle. If you have questions about a specific change, ask the front desk staff.

## Class Schedule

The class schedule is available on the website and filters automatically based on your selected location. You can see class names, times, instructors, and available capacity.

### iCal Feed

You can subscribe to the class schedule in your preferred calendar app. The iCal feed provides a live-updating calendar that reflects any schedule changes automatically.

To subscribe:

- **Google Calendar** — Add by URL using the iCal feed link
- **Apple Calendar** — Subscribe to a new calendar using the feed URL
- **Outlook** — Add an internet calendar using the feed URL

Ask the front desk for the iCal feed link for your location. Once subscribed, class times, names, and instructor assignments stay current without any action on your part.

## Check-In

Every time you attend a class, you check in. There are three ways to do this:

### QR Code Scan

If you have your QR code (available on your dashboard or saved to your phone), scan it at the kiosk near the front desk. The kiosk confirms your check-in immediately.

### Name Search at Kiosk

At the front desk kiosk, use the name search to find yourself. Tap your name, select the class you are attending, and confirm.

### Staff Manual Check-In

If you forgot your phone or the kiosk is unavailable, ask a staff member. They can check you in from the admin system.

Each check-in is recorded and counts toward your badges, streaks, and promotion eligibility. Make sure you check in every time you train.

## Progress Tracking

Your training progress is visible on your dashboard and covers several areas.

### Belt Rank

Your current rank is displayed with your belt color and stripe count. Stripes are shown as filled dots (earned) and empty dots (remaining before the next belt level).

### Promotion Metrics

You can see:

- **Classes since last promotion** — how many classes you have attended at your current rank
- **Days at current rank** — how long you have held your current belt
- **Promotion thresholds** — the minimum classes and time required before you become eligible for the next rank

Meeting the thresholds does not guarantee a promotion — your instructor evaluates your readiness — but you can always see where you stand.

### Badges Page

Visit the Badges page to see all badges you have earned. Each badge includes a name, description, and the date you earned it. Your total badge count also appears on the dashboard.

## Badges

Badges are awarded automatically when you hit specific milestones. Once earned, a badge is yours permanently — badges are never removed.

### Badge Types

**Attendance Milestones** — Awarded when you reach a total class count:
- 1 class (First Class)
- 10 classes
- 25 classes
- 50 classes
- 100 classes
- 250 classes
- 500 classes

**Streak Milestones** — Awarded for consecutive-week streaks:
- 4 weeks
- 12 weeks
- 26 weeks

**Belt Promotion** — Awarded each time you are promoted to a new belt rank.

**Early Bird** — Awarded after 10 check-ins as the first person to check in for a class that day.

**Cross-Trainer** — Awarded when you are actively enrolled in 2 or more programs (e.g., BJJ and Kickboxing).

## Streaks

A streak tracks how many consecutive calendar weeks (Monday through Sunday) you attend at least one class.

### How Streaks Work

- Attend at least one class in a week to keep the streak alive
- Missing an entire week breaks your streak and the count resets to zero

### Streak Freezes

You have a limited number of freezes available per quarter. A freeze automatically preserves your streak through one missed week. If you miss a week and have a freeze available, it is used and your streak continues unbroken.

Freezes reset at the start of each calendar quarter (January, April, July, October).

### Streak Status

Your streak shows one of three statuses on your dashboard:

- **Active** — you attended class this week or the week is still in progress
- **Frozen** — you missed last week but a freeze was applied, preserving your streak
- **Broken** — you missed a week with no freeze available, and the streak reset

## Notifications

You can receive SMS notifications for important training events. To opt in, make sure your phone number is on your account.

Notifications are sent for:

- **Promotions** — when you earn a new belt rank
- **Badges earned** — when you unlock a new badge
- **Foundations cleared** — when you complete the Foundations program
- **Attendance milestones** — when you hit a class count milestone
- **Streak reminders** — if your streak is at risk of breaking

All SMS communication follows TCPA compliance. You can opt out at any time.

---

## Targeted Content

Some pages and posts on the website are only visible to members who meet certain criteria. Content can be restricted by:

- **Belt rank** — available only at or above a certain rank
- **Program** — visible only to members of a specific program
- **Location** — shown only to members at a specific location
- **Membership status** — available only to active members

If you cannot see something that another member can, it is likely because you have not yet met the criteria. As you progress in your training, more content becomes available to you. This is by design — it keeps information relevant to where you are in your journey.
